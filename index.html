Finding Faith I love my wife; her name is Faith, Faith Usher to be complete. I Love her so much she is so kind, she's such a good person, she's always there for me, always by my side. Her eyes are warm, her voice is sweet, her heart is open, and I really can't live without her. Today I came home from the office a little late and I couldn't find my Faith, I looked outside but I couldn't find her, I spoke to the neighbors around in the street " have you seen my Faith," I said, none could help me. I searched for my Faith I could not find her, I had lost my Faith, panicking I was asked every stranger, people I didn't know, nothing. Their eyes searching for the spark of uncertainty and a chance to take advantage of a weakness. They approach, with impending rage, their nostrils flaring. They collide, grappling for position, they struggle against each other, banging, scratching, and grunting. Letting their anger drive them, they compete for a glorious triumph and a customary howl. Then one turns, shoots, and scores.
