Goodmans Kosher

Rev. Sol Goodman and Goodmans Kosher Sol Goodman was a Jewish kosher ritual butcher in Houston, Texas in the early 1900's. He changed the kosher geography of Houston forever. With his soft approach, he brought kosher to those who were previously not interested in keeping kosher. It may be argued that Sol Goodman invented kiruv in Texas. The things Sol Goodman did He rented out the killing floor of a slaughterhouse, going there in the early hours of the morning before it was used. He gave up rabbinical studies to study for kabalah ordinatoin of shechita, when his father told him Houston needed an English speaking shochet. He was committed to the kashrus of Houston until he died. In all essence he was the Kashruth organization before there was an official one.
